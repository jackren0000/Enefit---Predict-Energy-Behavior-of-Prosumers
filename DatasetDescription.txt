The challenge in this competition is to predict electricity produced and consumed by Estonian energy customers,
who have installed solar panels. Having access to weather data, the relevant energy prices, and reccords of
installed photovoltaic capacity.

This is a forecasting competition using the time series API. The private leaderboard will be determined using real
data gathered after ther submission period closes.

All datasets following the same time convention. Time is given in EET/EEST.
